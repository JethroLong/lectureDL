        • MM1 COURSE OUTLINE

          Attached Files:
          •  MM1 17S2 outline .pdf (93.626 KB)

          Dear Making Movies One Students, 

          Welcome to the wonderful world of making movies!

          This is the course outline for this semester. It contains the details of each session, when and where it takes place, and who delivers it. It also includes dates for submission of assessments, and a description of what these assessment are.

          PLEASE NOTE: THERE ARE TWO SESSION OF MAKING MOIVES ONE: Wednesdays from 2.15 to 4.15pm and Thursdays from 4pm to 6pm. You must choose which session you will attend and stay in that group for the entire semester. Switching between classes will not be accepted as both groups are full and the room has a limit to the number of people it can accommodate. Roles will be handed out at each class as well.

          Have a read - see you in the last week of July.

          HELEN GAYNOR

          Making Movies One Breadth Co-ordinator
